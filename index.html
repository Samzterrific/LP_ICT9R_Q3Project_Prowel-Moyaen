Welcome to SMT Express!

The Manila Metro Rail Transit (MRT) and the Manila Light Rail Transit System (MLT) are rapid transit systems in Manila, Philippines. The MRT features one line with 13 stations, while the MLT comprises two lines with 31 stations. The MRT started operating in December 1999, whereas the MLT began service in December 1984.
